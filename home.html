Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

  • Q: What is an online garage?

    A: An online garage is a digital platform where you can book various automotive services such as repairs, maintenance, and inspections for your vehicle.

  • Q: How does the online garage work?

    A: You can browse available services, select the one you need, book an appointment, and even make payments online. Our certified mechanics will then come to your location or you can visit our partnered garages.

Booking and Appointments

  • Q: How do I book a service?

    A: Simply visit our website, choose the service you need, select your preferred date and time, and complete the booking process.

  • Q: Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

    A: Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment through your account on our website. Please refer to our cancellation policy for more details.

  • Q: Do I need to create an account to book a service?

    A: Yes, creating an account allows you to track your appointments, manage bookings, and access service history.

Services Offered

  • Q: What types of services do you offer?

    A: We offer a wide range of services including oil changes, brake repairs, tire services, engine diagnostics, and more.

  • Q: Are your mechanics certified?

    A: Yes, all our mechanics are certified and have extensive experience in automotive repair and maintenance.

  • Q: Do you offer warranty on your services?

    A: Yes, we offer a warranty on all our services. Please refer to our warranty policy for specific details

Pricing and Payments

  • Q: How much do your services cost?
